  • How do I Create a Fundraiser?

    Creating a fundraiser is easy. After you login, the system will take you to the "My Account" Page. On that page, you can click the blue "Create Fundraiser" button. Enter all the fundraiser details and click submit for approval. Fundraiser's are usually approved within 24 hours. It's that simple.

  • How do I join a Fundraiser?

    Your coach or fundraising leader will give you a six-digit fundraiser join code. You will need to login to the website and click the blue "Join Fundraiser" button. That is where you will be able to enter your join code and become a participant of the fundraiser.
